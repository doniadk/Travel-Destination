About Norway

Location Scandinavian Peninsula, Northern Europe
Geography Varied terrain including mountains, fjords, forests, and coastline
Climate Varies from temperate along the coast to subarctic and arctic inland
Population Approximately 5.4 million (as of 2021)
Language Norwegian (Bokmål and Nynorsk)

Explore the stunning landscapes, rich culture, and vibrant cities of Norway. From the majestic fjords to the northern lights dancing across the Arctic sky, Norway offers an unforgettable experience for travelers.
