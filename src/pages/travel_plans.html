Oslo - City Sightseeing Tour

Description: Explore the highlights of Oslo on this guided sightseeing tour. Visit iconic landmarks such as the Viking Ship Museum, Oslo Opera House, and Vigeland Sculpture Park. Learn about the city's rich history and vibrant culture from knowledgeable guides.

  • Duration: Approximately 3 hours
  • Inclusions: Guided tour, transportation, admission fees
  • Price: $50 per person
  • Additional Information: Tours available daily, with multiple departure times. Suitable for all ages and fitness levels.

Bergen - Fjord Cruise Tour

Description: Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Norwegian fjords on this scenic cruise from Bergen. Sail through picturesque fjords, admire towering mountains and cascading waterfalls, and explore charming coastal villages along the way.

  • Duration: Full-day excursion
  • Inclusions: Cruise tickets, onboard refreshments, guided commentary
  • Price: $100 per person
  • Additional Information: Tours operate seasonally, with limited availability. Don't forget your camera to capture stunning views along the route.

Tromsø - Northern Lights Tour

Description: Embark on an unforgettable journey to witness the mesmerizing Northern Lights in Tromsø. Venture into the Arctic wilderness, far from city lights, and marvel at nature's spectacular light show dancing across the night sky.

  • Duration: Evening excursion
  • Inclusions: Transportation, expert guide, thermal clothing, hot beverages
  • Price: $150 per person
  • Additional Information: Tours are weather-dependent and subject to Aurora forecasts. Warm clothing and sturdy footwear are recommended for outdoor viewing.
Book Now

Description: Discover the enchanting beauty of the Lofoten Islands on this island-hopping adventure. Explore quaint fishing villages, hike scenic trails, and soak in panoramic views of rugged coastlines and pristine beaches.

  • Duration: Full-day tour
  • Inclusions: Transportation between islands, guided hiking tour, picnic lunch
  • Price: $120 per person
  • Additional Information: Tours operate in small groups for a personalized experience. Suitable for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts of all levels.
