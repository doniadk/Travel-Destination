Overview

Tromsø is located in the Arctic Circle, making it an ideal destination for experiencing the Northern Lights and the midnight sun. It offers stunning natural landscapes, exciting outdoor activities, and a vibrant cultural scene.

Visitors to Tromsø can enjoy activities such as whale watching, dog sledding, and snowmobiling, as well as exploring the city's historic landmarks and cultural attractions.
