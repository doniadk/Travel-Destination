Overview

Oslo is the capital city of Norway, located on the southern coast at the head of the Oslofjord. It is known for its rich history, vibrant cultural scene, and stunning natural beauty.

The city offers a wide range of attractions, including historic landmarks, museums, parks, and scenic waterfronts. From exploring the Viking Ship Museum to strolling through the Royal Palace Park, there's something for everyone to enjoy in Oslo.

Attractions

Viking Ship Museum

Explore the well-preserved Viking ships and artifacts.

Oslo Opera House

Experience world-class performances and stunning architecture.

Vigeland Sculpture Park

Admire the impressive collection of sculptures by Gustav Vigeland.

Akershus Fortress

Discover the medieval fortress with panoramic views of the city.
