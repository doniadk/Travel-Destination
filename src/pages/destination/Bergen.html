Overview

Bergen is a picturesque coastal city in Norway, surrounded by majestic fjords and steep mountains. It is known for its colorful wooden houses in the Bryggen wharf area, the Fløibanen funicular, and the nearby Hardangerfjord.

The city offers a charming blend of history, culture, and natural beauty, making it a popular destination for travelers seeking adventure and relaxation.

Attractions

Bryggen Wharf

Explore the historic Bryggen Wharf, known for its colorful wooden houses and rich maritime heritage.

Fløibanen Funicular

Experience breathtaking views of Bergen from atop Mount Fløyen by riding the iconic Fløibanen Funicular.

Hanseatic Museum

Dive into Bergen's Hanseatic history at the Hanseatic Museum, housed in a preserved merchant's house from the 18th century.

Fish Market

Indulge in the fresh flavors of Bergen at its vibrant Fish Market, offering a variety of seafood delicacies and local specialties
